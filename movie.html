Kolaisevel

2026


The story follows a young married couple, Kaali (Kalaiyarasan) and Anusuya (Deepa Balu). Following a 200-year-old family tradition for pregnant women, the family travels to an ancestral temple deep within the Javvadu Hills for a pregnancy ritual. While the first half plays out as a realistic family drama, the narrative shifts into a dark thriller as the couple encounters unexpected danger rooted in a caste-related "honour killing" plot.
